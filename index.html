Complete guide to hospitalization records in the MIS

April 5, 2026 · 28 min read · Applies to: h24.ua MIS

The Hospitalization records module is the core of daily inpatient work in the MIS. It tracks every active and historical episode — from admission through ward transfers and procedure records to final discharge. This guide covers the complete workflow.

Before you start

You need at minimum a Ward Clerk or Attending Physician role in the MIS. Read-only users can view records but cannot create or edit episodes.

Navigating to the hospitalization registry

From the main menu, go to Стаціонар → Журнал госпіталізації. The registry opens showing the current day's active admissions by default. The toolbar at the top controls filtering, date range, and export options.

The hospitalization registry — departments listed on the left, episode list on the right

Setting a date range filter

By default the registry shows only today. To pull historical data for a report:

  • 1Click the calendar icon in the top toolbar. A date-picker modal appears with "From" and "To" fields.
  • 2Enter your start date in "Від" and end date in "До". The system accepts DD.MM.YYYY format.
  • 3Click Застосувати. The table re-loads with all episodes that fall within the selected range.
  • 4Optionally narrow further by department using the left sidebar. Selected departments highlight in blue; deselect to include all.
Performance note

Ranges longer than 90 days may take 10–20 seconds to load under high server load. For large exports, use the background job option under Звіти → Планові завдання instead of the live registry.

Exporting records to Excel

After filtering the date range and departments, click Експорт → Excel in the top-right of the toolbar. A dialog allows you to choose which columns to include in the export file.

Export config Selected columns for standard monthly report: ✓ № картки ✓ ПІБ пацієнта ✓ Дата народження ✓ Дата надходження ✓ Дата виписки ✓ Ліжко-дні ✓ Відділення ✓ Основний діагноз (ICD-10) ✗ Лікар (attending — exclude for privacy) ✗ Номер палати (room — optional) Output: .xlsx · Encoding: UTF-8 · Max rows: 10 000
Tip

If the export button is greyed out, your session may have timed out. Refresh the page, re-apply your filters, and try again. MIS sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity.
